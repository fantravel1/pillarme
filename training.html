Zero To Hero AI Program

Master Working with AI in Less than 24 Hours

The AI revolution is not linear — it is exponential. What took companies years to build five years ago can now be prototyped in hours. The Zero To Hero AI Program puts you on the right side of this exponential curve. You will go from zero AI experience to confidently building AI-powered tools, workflows, and products in a single day.

Think about the compounding effect: if AI capability doubles every few months, the gap between those who understand it and those who don't grows not by addition but by multiplication. Participants who completed this program have gone on to launch AI-powered businesses, automate entire departments, and create products reaching millions — all within weeks of finishing. The difference between starting today and starting six months from now isn't six months of progress. It's an entire generation of capability.

  • Build and deploy AI-powered tools and workflows from scratch
  • Automate complex processes that previously required entire teams
  • Understand prompt engineering, model selection, and AI architecture
  • Create AI products that serve real users on day one

Advanced Problem Solving

Beyond Design Thinking

Design thinking opened the door. Advanced Problem Solving walks through it and keeps going. This program teaches you to dismantle complex, entrenched problems — the kind that have resisted conventional approaches for years — and replace them with breakthrough solutions that actually ship.

Our graduates have tackled problems that Fortune 500 consultants couldn't crack: redesigning healthcare delivery systems, creating financial inclusion tools for underserved populations, building education platforms that reach rural communities across continents, and launching advocacy campaigns that shifted public policy. The method works because it combines rapid prototyping, systems-level thinking, and relentless bias toward action. You won't just analyze the problem. You will solve it and launch the solution before the workshop is over.

  • Dismantle complex, systemic problems using structured breakthrough frameworks
  • Move from insight to shipped solution in days, not quarters
  • Combine systems thinking with rapid prototyping for real-world impact
  • Build solutions that scale across organizations and communities
