The Growth Story

From Domains to a Digital Travel Empire

The Travel Experience vertical began as a curated collection of premium domain names spanning sports fan travel, cultural tourism, and destination brands. Today, it operates as a standalone media vertical with 100+ live content properties generating tens of millions of monthly pageviews.

Led by flagship property FanTravel.com, the vertical has proven the PILLAR thesis: premium domains, combined with AI-powered content and strategic SEO, create category-defining digital properties. The travel vertical is our proof of concept at scale.
