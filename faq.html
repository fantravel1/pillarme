  • Media Holdings: Digital media properties and content platforms
  • Premium Domains: One of the world's largest portfolios of premium domain names

  • Custom Development: Professional services for bespoke integration requirements

Our integration team provides technical documentation, sandbox environments, and dedicated support throughout the implementation process.

Domains

4 questions

PILLAR operates a professional domain partnership model that provides immediate access to premium domain names without the substantial capital investment typically required for outright acquisition:

  • Immediate Access: Begin using your selected premium domain within days, not months
  • Full Operational Control: Configure DNS, email, and hosting to your specifications
  • Technical Management: PILLAR handles domain registration, renewals, and security
  • Flexible Terms: Monthly, annual, or multi-year agreements available
  • Upgrade Path: Options to transition to ownership for qualifying clients

This model enables organizations to establish authoritative digital presence on premium domains while preserving capital for core business operations.

PILLAR manages one of the world's largest portfolios of premium domain names, with 7,000+ digital properties spanning multiple categories:

  • Industry Verticals: Finance, healthcare, technology, travel, real estate
  • Generic Keywords: High-value single and two-word domains
  • Geographic: City, region, and country-specific domains
  • Brand-Ready: Domains suitable for new brand development
  • Exact Match: Domains matching high-volume search queries

Browse our domain portfolio or contact our team for assistance identifying domains aligned with your business objectives.

PILLAR provides clear, transparent terms for agreement conclusion with multiple options:

  • Renewal: Continue your agreement with consistent terms and pricing
  • Upgrade: Transition to a higher-tier package with additional services
  • Domain Transfer: For qualifying long-term clients, purchase options may be available
  • Graceful Exit: 90-day transition period to migrate content and redirect traffic

We work collaboratively with all clients to ensure smooth transitions regardless of the path chosen, with dedicated support throughout the process.

Pricing

4 questions

PILLAR offers four structured solution packages designed for organizations at different stages of growth:

  • Foundation ($125/month): Premium domain partnership, professional website, SSL certificate, basic analytics
  • Growth ($425/month): Everything in Foundation plus AI content generation, SEO optimization, monthly reporting
  • Authority ($1,000/month): Everything in Growth plus priority support, custom integrations, white-glove onboarding
  • Enterprise (Custom): Tailored solutions with dedicated account team, SLA guarantees, custom development

All pricing is based on annual agreements. Monthly billing is available at adjusted rates. View detailed pricing or contact us for a custom quote.

PILLAR maintains transparent, predictable pricing with no hidden fees:

  • Setup Fees: None for Foundation, Growth, and Authority packages
  • Domain Costs: Included in all packages (no separate registration fees)
  • SSL Certificates: Included at no additional cost
  • Support: Standard support included; priority support in Authority and above
  • Renewals: Automatic at agreed rates with advance notification

Enterprise engagements may include one-time implementation fees for custom development work, which are clearly outlined in proposals before agreement execution.

  • Commitment Discounts: Additional savings for multi-year agreements

Contact our enterprise team to discuss volume pricing aligned with your portfolio size and business requirements.

PILLAR offers flexible payment options to accommodate global business operations:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express (automated billing)
