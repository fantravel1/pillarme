üåê Domain Questions

How does domain rental work? ‚ñº

Instead of buying a domain outright for thousands of dollars, you rent premium domains starting at $25/month.

Your domain stays active as long as you maintain your subscription. Cancel anytime with no long-term commitment. It's like leasing a premium address instead of buying real estate.

What happens if I cancel my domain rental? ‚ñº

If you cancel:

  • Your website will go offline at the end of your billing period
  • The domain returns to our marketplace for others to rent
  • You keep all your original content and can export your website files
  • No cancellation fees or penalties

We recommend giving us 30 days notice so we can help you transition smoothly.

Can I buy the domain outright? ‚ñº

Yes! After renting for 12 months, you have the option to purchase your domain at a discounted rate. The purchase price varies by domain, but your rental payments may be partially credited toward the purchase.

Contact us to discuss domain purchase options.

Why are some domains more expensive? ‚ñº

Domain pricing is based on:

  • $25/month - Standard premium domains
  • $100/month - Exceptional SEO potential, shorter length, or highly brandable
  • $500-$1000/month - Ultra-premium domains that are already developed with content, or extremely valuable one-word domains

Premium domains often have better SEO potential and are more memorable for your audience.

Can I use my own domain instead? ‚ñº

Absolutely! If you already own a domain, we can point it to your PILLAR-built website. Just select "I have my own domain" during the intake process, and we'll provide DNS configuration instructions.
