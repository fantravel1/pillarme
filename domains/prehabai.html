Frequently Asked Questions

What is prehabilitation?
Prehabilitation (or "prehab") is proactive healthcare that prepares the body before surgery, athletic activity, or other physical challenges. It focuses on preventing injuries and complications rather than treating them after they occur. Studies show prehab can reduce surgical complications by 50% and accelerate recovery times.
Why combine AI with prehabilitation?
AI can analyze movement patterns, predict injury risks, personalize exercise programs, and track progress more effectively than traditional methods. Machine learning models can identify subtle biomechanical issues that human observers might miss, making prevention more precise and effective. PrehabAI.com perfectly positions a platform at this intersection.
What's the market opportunity for prehab services?
The global preventive healthcare market is experiencing explosive growth, projected to reach $330 billion by 2027. Healthcare systems increasingly recognize that preventing injuries and complications is more cost-effective than treatment. Insurance companies are beginning to cover prehab services, creating a massive addressable market.
