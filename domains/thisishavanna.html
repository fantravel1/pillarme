Havana Tourism Market

Havana welcomes over 4 million visitors annually, drawn by unique attractions:

  • Old Havana (Habana Vieja): UNESCO World Heritage colonial architecture
  • Classic Cars: Iconic vintage American automobiles from the 1950s
  • Music & Dance: Salsa, rumba, and live music venues throughout the city
  • Cuban Cigars: World-famous tobacco culture and cigar rolling experiences
  • Rum & Cocktails: Mojitos, daiquiris, and rum distillery tours
  • Art & Culture: Vibrant street art, galleries, and revolutionary history
