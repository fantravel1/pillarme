Frequently Asked Questions

What is PMDD?
PMDD (Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder) is a severe form of premenstrual syndrome affecting 5-8% of menstruating individuals. It causes significant emotional, physical, and behavioral symptoms during the luteal phase of the menstrual cycle. Unlike PMS, PMDD can dramatically impact work, relationships, and daily functioning, and is recognized as a medical condition requiring specialized treatment.
