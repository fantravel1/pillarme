Partnership Structure Options

Modern domain partnership agreements accommodate sophisticated business needs:

Standard Partnership

  • Fixed monthly or annual payments
  • 1-5% of domain value annually
  • Full DNS and email control
  • 99.99% uptime guarantees

Revenue Share Models

  • Base fee plus percentage of revenue
  • Aligns costs with business success
  • Lower initial payments
  • Scales with growth

Lease-to-Own Options

  • Exit options at milestones
