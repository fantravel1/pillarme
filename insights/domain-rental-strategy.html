Rental Structure Options

Modern domain rental agreements accommodate sophisticated business needs:

Standard Rental

  • Fixed monthly or annual payments
  • 1-5% of domain value annually
  • Full DNS and email control

Revenue Share Models

Rent-to-Own Options
